Do I need an appointment?

We, at Valuprep, value your time just as much as we value ours. We recommend that you make an Appointment before you come see us so that we can provide you the best customer service that you deserve.

What should I prepare prior to my appointment?

For your ease, we have put together a Client Information Form that you can fill out and print prior to coming in and meeting with us. By no means do we expect you to have everything completed on this form. However, the more prepared you are and the information you can provide us by completing the form, the more we can ensure that we have thoroughly taken advantage of all the tax savings opportunities you may have available to you. (link to Client Information Form here)

What should I bring with me?

Please bring the following documents with you to your appointment. The list is not all-inclusive. It is a list of common documents needed to prepare the tax returns. Additional documentation may be requested as needed.

Copy of prior year tax-returns (new clients only)

W-2s (including spouse and dependent children W-2)

1099s

Mortgage Statements

Retirement Savings Statements (e.g. IRA and Roth IRA)

Real Estate Tax paid during the year

Bank Interest Statements

Brokerage Statements

Documentation (e.g. check copies) of Estimated Income Taxes paid to federal, state and local governments for current tax-year

Documentation (e.g. check copies) of Estimated Income Taxes paid to federal, state and local governments for current tax-year

Charitable contributions (cash and non-cash)

Schedule K-1's

Closing Statements if you purchased or sold a home during the current tax-year

Documentation of any other self-employment income (if any)

Documentation related to rental property (if any)

How much will it cost to have my income tax return prepared?

We strive to prepare accurate tax returns at the most reasonable fee possible. We base our fee on the tax forms and schedules we need to prepare for accurate completion of your income tax return. We would be glad to provide you a no-obligations fee estimate. If you make an appointment with us, we can then review all of your information in a one-on-one session (preferably in person but not necessary), determine the work that we think will be involved, and then provide you a fee estimate. You can also take advantage of our Special Offers.

When can I expect my refund?

You can use the below link to track your Federal Income Tax Refund by providing your social Security #, filing status, and exact refund amount expected.

Federal Refund Status

https://irs.gov/refundstatus

Tracking State Income Tax refund (s) differs by state; however, below is the link to track your income tax refund for the State of Ohio.

Ohio Refund Status

https://www.tax.state.oh.us/WheresMyRefundWeb
