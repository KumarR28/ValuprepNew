Our Services

"Your Tax Returns Done Right The First Time"

Individual Income Tax Preparation

We provide federal, state, and local income tax preparation services for individuals and their small businesses. By keeping our focus on individual taxes, we can ensure that we are maintaining and offering our taxation expertise to this select group of tax clients rather than trying to be everything to everybody.

We will utilize our extensive knowledge of new tax laws to prepare the best tax return possible for you. We can identify all tax planning opportunities that reduce your current tax liabilities and also identify and implement ways to minimize your future tax liabilities. For example, we can review your current payroll withholdings based on your unique situation and recommends suggestions to optimize your future withholdings from you paychecks.

Prior-Year Tax Return Review

We can not only prepare your current year tax returns, but we can review your prior year tax returns that were prepared using an online tax prep service, a franchise tax prep service, or another accountant to see if they were accurately prepared. We will review these returns to see if there were any errors, ommissions, or missed deduction and credit opportunities.
